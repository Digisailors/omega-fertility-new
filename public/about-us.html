About Omega

Omega Fertility & Test Tube Baby Centre, A Unit of Sundaram Arulrhaj Hospitals, Tuticorin is a complete fertility centre founded in March 2015, to provide high-quality fertility care with an established, success-proven, internationally trained medical team and a promise to provide the very best care in order to deliver a positive outcome. Dr. Archana Ambujan, Director of the centre has got specialized training in infertility at various infertility institutes in India and abroad. With a dedicated and hardworking teams she is fulfilling the dream of childless couples by providing comprehensive services in IUI, IVF, ICSI, Frozen Embryos, Donor Egg and Donor Sperm apart from complete Woman health care under the name of Omega Fertility & Test Tube Baby Centre.

The dream of a complete family if not fulfilled is a devastating experience. The state of the art IVF Centre of this hospital combines quality, compassion, integrity, responsibility, and accountability in equal measures and is providing Assisted Reproduction Techniques of International standards within the reach of every individual. We provide couples a World-class Test tube baby & Fertility treatment and specialized infertility investigations all under one roof.Our aim is to help childless couples build a healthy family as efficiently, quickly and cost-effectively as possible. Infertility treatment with a comprehensive and flexible service of the highest standard in rural areas of southern districts of Tamilnadu has been made possible by our Centre. We provide comprehensive and fruitful management of infertility.

A highly reputed and distinguished, multi-specialty hospital, Sundaram Arulrhaj Hospitals is equipped with modern facilities to provide intensive Medical care. From years of experience, learning, and research, the hospital has developed very effective management strategies for the diagnosis and treatment of various ailments. The team of highly competent and experienced doctors are committed to giving total care to the patients. Omega Fertility & Test Tube Baby Centre is another feather in the cap of the latest treatment service of Sundaram Arulrhaj Hospitals.
