Ovulation Induction (OI)

Ovulation Induction (OI) is the controlled administration of drugs to stimulate the ovaries of the woman to produce mature good quality eggs.

Ovulation Induction is the simplest and the primary treatment of infertile couples. OI is typically applied to couples in whom the woman has irregular menstrual cycles and in whom investigations indicate that they may not be ovulating on a regular basis such as PCOS conditions. OI may also be the treatment for couples who have mild problems leading to infertility.

Treatment

  • Thorough Investigation is carried out
  • Blood samples are collected at various stages of the cycle to measure the hormone level of the female
  • Transvaginal Ultrasound is done to check for follicles & thickness of womb’s lining
  • Medication is prescribed as per the requirement
